About Me

Hello! I'm Jackson Pipe, a Software Developer based in Hamilton, ON, with a passion for creating innovative and efficient software solutions. My journey in the tech world spans over 3 years, where I've honed my skills in full-stack development, network security, and Agile methodologies.


As a graduate of Niagara College with a Diploma in Computer Programming, I've immersed myself in a range of technologies and languages, including C#, C++, Python, JavaScript, React, and Node.js, among others. My educational background and hands-on experience have equipped me to design, test, and implement complex web applications that drive results and exceed expectations.


My commitment goes beyond just coding. As a Coding Instructor, I had the rewarding experience of igniting the spark of programming in young minds, teaching languages like Scratch, Python, and Java. This role enhanced my communication skills and allowed me to simplify complex concepts for easy understanding.


In my spare time, I'm either exploring the latest technology trends or contributing to my GitHub projects. I believe in continuous learning and staying updated with the ever-evolving tech landscape.


I am enthusiastic about bringing my blend of coding skills and creative problem-solving to a forward-thinking team where I can continue to contribute to meaningful projects and grow as a professional.
